1. Controller

MusicMedia, based in Amsterdam, is responsible for processing personal data as described in this policy. Contact: privacy@musicmedia.nl.

2. Personal Data We Process

  • Identification: name, email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Contact details: optional phone number, address (for billing)
  • Payment details: only via certified payment providers, never stored on our servers
  • Usage data: listening behavior, preferences, uploads, interactions, feedback
  • Technical data: IP address, browser, device, cookies, log files

3. Purposes and Legal Grounds

  • Performance of the contract (service provision, subscriptions, billing)
  • Personalization of recommendations and user experience
  • Compliance with legal obligations (tax, retention)
  • Security, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting
  • Marketing (only with explicit consent)

4. Retention Periods

  • Data is not kept longer than strictly necessary or legally required
  • Account data: up to 1 year after termination, unless longer required by law
  • Billing data: 7 years in accordance with tax law
  • Cookies: up to 12 months

5. Sharing with Third Parties

  • Only with parties that comply with the GDPR and solely for the purposes mentioned above
  • Payment providers, hosting, analytics, email services (all with data processing agreements)
  • Personal data is never sold to third parties
  • Data may be processed outside the EU, but only if appropriate safeguards are in place (such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses)

6. Cookies & Tracking

  • Functional cookies are necessary for the operation of the site
  • Analytical and marketing cookies are only placed with explicit consent
  • You can always adjust your preferences via the cookie settings

7. Your Rights

  • Right to access, correct, delete, port, restrict, and object to your data
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to human intervention in automated decision-making
  • Complaints can be submitted to the Dutch Data Protection Authority

8. Security

  • Advanced technical and organizational security measures
  • Encryption of passwords and sensitive data
  • Access control and logging of data access
  • Regular security audits and updates

9. Minors

  • Users under 16 require parental or guardian consent. We may request proof of consent and block accounts if it is missing.
  • Parents are advised to supervise their children's use of MusicMedia.
  • Minors have the same privacy rights as adults, but parents can submit requests on behalf of their child.

10. Changes & Contact

  • Changes to this policy will be communicated in a timely manner
  • Last update: 2 May 2025
  • Questions or requests? Email privacy@musicmedia.nl
