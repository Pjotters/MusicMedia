2. Payments

  • Payments are processed securely via certified payment providers (iDEAL, credit card, PayPal, etc.).

3. Cancellation & Changes

  • You can change or cancel your subscription at any time free of charge via your account.
  • Upon cancellation, the subscription stops immediately; no refund is given for the current month unless required by law.
  • You have a 14-day cooling-off period after purchasing a paid subscription (European consumer law).

4. Discounts & Promotions

  • Discounts or promotions are clearly communicated and valid for a limited time.
  • Promotions are not cumulative unless otherwise stated.

5. Minors

  • Minors (under 16) require parental or guardian consent to purchase a paid subscription.
  • We may request proof of consent and block subscriptions if it is missing.
  • Parents are advised to supervise their children's use of MusicMedia.

6. Questions or Complaints

  • For questions about prices, billing, or your subscription, contact support@musicmedia.nl.
  • Complaints are handled within 14 days, in accordance with our complaints procedure.
